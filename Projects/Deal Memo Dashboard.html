Deal Memo Dashboard

Developed Deal Memo Dashboard using Microsoft Power Platform as part of Microsoft 365!  Leveraging Power Automate and SharePoint, this solution automates the Deal Memo approval process by enabling seamless submission of investment proposals. Automated email notifications are triggered for each of the three approval stages (CRO, CIO, ED), ensuring smooth approvals and maintaining an audit trail. All processes are seamlessly integrated within the company's intranet, enhancing efficiency and transparency.
